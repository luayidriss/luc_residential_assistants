What is an RA?

Residential Assistants (RAs for short) are trained students that facilitate in creating a safe and inclusive environement in the college residence.


What does an RA do?

RAs are part of the student support system at Leiden Univeristy College. Their role is made up of two components: to be the first point of contact to student’s issues, and to facilitate organizing community events. They are trained in dealing with emergency and non-emergency situations. RAs are trained in:


  • First Aid and Fire
  • Mental Health First Aid
  • Grief Management
  • Drug-related First Aid
  • Suicide Prevention
  • Event Management
  • Active Listenining
  • Inclusivity
  • Boundaries and Sex-Ed
